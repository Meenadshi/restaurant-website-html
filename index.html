About Us

We've been making healthy food last for 50 years!

One of India's most beloved restaurants, M & N has welcomed guests to enjoy its delicious Indian cuisine, warm hospitality, and unparalleled service in the Chennai City for over two decades. Our restaurant's ever-evolving seasonal menu showcases our relationships with local farms and purveyors. Opened in 1994 by restaurateur Naveen Kumar in the busy place of T-Nagar, the restaurant has earned many awards and recognition for its outstanding service!
